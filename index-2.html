🔗 What we check against

PIB Fact Check, Press Council of India, Election Commission statements, AFP India, AltNews, BoomLive, Vishvasnews, and 40+ more verified sources.

🤖 How the AI Score Works

Our NLP engine checks for emotional manipulation, unverifiable claims, missing attributions, and cross-references the content with known fact-checked articles. Score 0–40 = Likely False, 41–70 = Misleading, 71–100 = Credible.

🔒 Your Privacy

Submissions are anonymous. We don't store personal data. Claims are only used to improve detection accuracy — never shared or sold.

⚡ Need Urgent Verification?

During elections and communal events, we prioritize high-reach claims. Flag your submission as "Urgent" and our team reviews it within 30 minutes.

How TruthGuard Works

1

Submit

Paste any suspicious claim, news headline, or viral message in any Indian language.

2

AI Analysis

Our NLP engine checks for manipulation tactics, emotional triggers, and suspicious patterns in milliseconds.

3

Source Check
